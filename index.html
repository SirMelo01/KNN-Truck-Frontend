Compensation Eligibility

Who can claim compensation?

You are eligible if you purchased, leased, or financed new trucks from Scania, Daimler/Mercedes, MAN, Volvo/Renault, DAF, or Iveco between 1997 and 2016. Whether you own a single truck or an entire fleet, you may have a valid claim.
